# Research and Problem Understanding

## Problem Statement

During disasters, emergency reports arrive through:

- Voice messages
- Text messages
- Images
- Handwritten notes

These reports are difficult to organize and prioritize.

---

## Existing Solutions

### Government Helplines

Require internet and manual processing.

### Cloud AI Systems

Require internet and powerful servers.

---

## Problem Gaps

- Not offline.
- Privacy concerns.
- High infrastructure requirements.

---

## Proposed Solution

RescueSync AI:

Input:
Audio, Image, Text

Output:
Structured incident reports.

Storage:
SQLite.

Inference:
CPU only.

---

## Target Users

- Disaster Management Teams
- NGOs
- Government Agencies
- Relief Volunteers

---

## Expected Benefits

- Faster response.
- Better prioritization.
- Offline operation.
- Improved data organization.
